What makes it different

One panel for what used to take a pile of apps and devices.

Recording a screen, a webcam, a phone, and a couple of mics normally means a separate tool for each — then wrestling the files together afterward. Capture runs every stream from a single panel, recording them at once while keeping each as its own independent track.

Every stream, its own track

Webcam, laptop screen, an external monitor, a single browser tab, even a connected phone — each is an independent video track you start, pause, and finish on its own.

Audio you can actually separate

Microphone, external mic, and desktop/system audio as its own track — not baked into the video. Live VU meters on every channel so you can see what's hot.

Export your way

Single — one video plus all audio. Export All — every track on its own. Merge — assign one or more audio tracks to each video before you render.

Runs in your browser. No install, no account, nothing uploaded — recording stays on your machine.
